Tips for Smart Bike Ownership

Down Payment Matters

A higher down payment reduces your loan amount and monthly EMI. Aim for at least 25% of the bike price.

Fuel Efficiency Tips

Regular maintenance, proper tire pressure, and smooth riding can improve mileage by 10-15%.

Additional Costs

Remember to factor in insurance, registration, maintenance, and accessories in your budget.
