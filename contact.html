Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about MrBike

How do I report incorrect information on the website?

If you find any incorrect information (prices, specifications, dealer details, etc.), please use the contact form above and select "Information Correction" as the subject. Include the specific page URL and the correct information so we can update it promptly.

How can I suggest a new bike to be added to the website?

We're always looking to expand our database! Send us a message with the bike details (brand, model, specifications, and official website link if available) and we'll add it to our research queue. You can also mention if it's a newly launched model in Bangladesh.

I'm a dealer. How can I get my showroom listed?

Dealers can request listing by contacting us via email at dealers@MrBike.com. Please include your showroom name, address, contact information, brands you carry, and any website/social media links. We verify all dealer information before listing.

How often is the information on MrBike updated?

We update our database regularly, with price information being checked monthly and specifications updated as soon as new information is available from official sources. However, prices and specifications can change rapidly, so we recommend verifying critical information with official dealers.

Can I contribute articles or reviews to MrBike?

Yes! We welcome contributions from motorcycle enthusiasts and experts. Please send your article/review idea to content@MrBike.com with a brief outline and your background/experience. We review all submissions and will get back to you if it aligns with our content strategy.

Is MrBike affiliated with any motorcycle brands?

MrBike is an independent platform and not directly affiliated with any motorcycle manufacturer. We aim to provide unbiased information to help consumers make informed decisions. Some brands may advertise on our platform, but this doesn't affect our editorial content.
