Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 5, 2025

Mr BikeBD is a motorcycle information website operating under our parent company, UNLEFT. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect information related to our website visitors and services.

1. Who We Are

Mr BikeBD is dedicated to providing comprehensive, accurate, and up-to-date information about motorcycles available in Bangladesh. We focus on bike specs, prices, trending and upcoming models, and showroom listings.

2. Future Features

In the near future, we plan to launch a feature allowing users to post and sell used bikes directly on our website. This will involve user-submitted content, which will be subject to additional terms and conditions when released.

3. What Information We Show

The information displayed on our site is collected from publicly available sources including:

  • Google search results
  • YouTube reviews and channels
  • Official brand websites
  • ChatGPT summaries and general public knowledge

We aim to ensure accuracy but cannot guarantee that all information is 100% error-free.

4. Showroom Listings

We currently list both official and unofficial bike showrooms based on data collected through online research, mostly from Google Maps and search results. If you are a showroom owner and wish to correct or update your listing, please contact us directly.

5. Personal Data

At this time, Mr BikeBD does not collect any personal data from visitors. We do not use cookies, tracking tools, or user accounts. When the used-bike posting feature launches, appropriate data handling and privacy terms will be added.

6. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or wish to correct or remove information listed on our site, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@mrbikebd.com
  • Phone: 018xxxxxxxx

We are committed to protecting your privacy and continuously improving your experience on Mr BikeBD.
