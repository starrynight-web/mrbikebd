MrBike is a motorcycle information website operating under our parent company, UNLEFT. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect information related to our website visitors and services.

About This Policy

This policy applies to all information collected through our website and services. We are committed to being transparent about our data practices.

1

Who We Are

MrBike is dedicated to providing comprehensive, accurate, and up-to-date information about motorcycles available in Bangladesh. Our mission is to help motorcycle enthusiasts, buyers, and riders make informed decisions.

2

Future Features

We plan to allow users to post and sell used bikes directly on our website in the near future. When this feature launches, we will update this privacy policy to include information about data collection and usage specific to the used bike marketplace.

Coming Soon: Used Bike Marketplace

We're developing a platform where users can safely buy and sell used motorcycles. This feature will have its own terms and privacy guidelines to protect both buyers and sellers.

3

What Information We Show

Our website displays motorcycle information collected from various public sources. We strive to provide accurate and current information, but we cannot guarantee 100% accuracy at all times.

Information Sources Include:

  • Google Search Results

    Publicly available information from across the web

  • YouTube Reviews and Channels

    Expert analysis and user experiences

  • Official Brand Websites

    Manufacturer specifications and announcements

  • ChatGPT Summaries

    AI-assisted research and public knowledge organization

Accuracy Disclaimer

Accuracy is prioritized but not guaranteed to be 100% error-free. Motorcycle specifications, prices, and availability change frequently. We recommend verifying critical information with official dealers before making purchasing decisions.

4

Showroom Listings

We list both official and unofficial showrooms based on online research. Our goal is to help users find motorcycle dealers and service centers across Bangladesh.

Showroom Information Updates

Showroom owners may contact us to update or correct listings. We verify information before updating our database.

Verification Process

We attempt to verify showroom information through multiple sources, but we cannot guarantee the accuracy of all listings.

5

Personal Data

MrBike does not collect personal data from website visitors. We believe in privacy by design and have implemented our services accordingly.

Our Privacy Commitment

No personal data collection - We don't ask for or store your personal information.
No cookies or tracking tools - We don't use cookies, analytics, or tracking technologies.
No user accounts required - All content is freely accessible without registration.

Future Data Collection

If we introduce features that require data collection (like the used bike marketplace), we will update this policy and implement appropriate privacy protections. Any data collection will be transparent, minimal, and for specific purposes only.
