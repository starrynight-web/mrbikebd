Section 2: Driving License Application Process

  • Fee Deposit: Visit a bank with your assessment slip and provide your mobile number.
  • Submit the money receipt and old blue-book photocopy to BRTA.
  • Receive an SMS for biometric submission.
  • Submit biometrics on the mentioned date.
  • Receive SMS for collecting RFID number plate and Smart Card.
  • Bring required docs & bike to collect card & plate.
  • Process usually takes about a month. For update, SMS: NP A 11 or call 6969.

Documents Required for License

  • Birth Certificate
  • NID / Voter ID
  • Passport (optional)
  • School/Educational Certificate

Bike Registration Process

  • Collect assessment slip
  • Pay registration fees
  • Submit documents, tax token, money receipt to BRTA
  • Verification by officer
  • Chassis inspection
  • Data entry into BRTA
  • Notification for plate issued
  • Collect plate & card on assigned date

Helpful Tips

      ✅ Double-check all documents.
        ✅ Use trusted dealer or agent if needed.
          ✅ Start early.
            ✅ Stay updated via BRTA website.

FAQs

Q: Can I register a second-hand bike?
A: Yes, with ownership transfer form & blue-book.
Q: Can I apply for license online?
A: Yes, via BRTA portal.
Q: Is insurance mandatory?
A: Yes, third-party insurance required.
Q: What if I don't register my bike?
A: Illegal. You may be fined or lose your bike.
Q: How long does registration take?
A: Usually 7–15 days.
