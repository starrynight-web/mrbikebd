Driving License Application Process

Step-by-step guide to getting your driving license in Bangladesh

1

Fee Deposit

Visit a bank with your assessment slip and provide your mobile number.

2

Submit Documents

Submit the money receipt and old blue-book photocopy to BRTA.

3

Biometric Submission

Receive an SMS for biometric submission and submit biometrics on the mentioned date.

4

Receive Collection SMS

Receive SMS for collecting RFID number plate and Smart Card.

5

Collect Card & Plate

Bring required documents & bike to collect card & plate. Process usually takes about a month.

Tip: For updates on your application, SMS: NP A 11 or call BRTA helpline at 6969.

Required Documents

Essential documents for license application and bike registration

For Driving License

  • Birth Certificate / National ID Card
  • Passport-sized photographs (4 copies)
  • Medical Certificate from registered doctor
  • Educational Certificate copy
  • Tax Token (for renewal applications)

For Bike Registration

  • Original Purchase Invoice from dealer
  • Insurance Certificate (Third Party)
  • Owner's National ID copy
  • Tax Payment Receipt
  • Previous Blue Book (for transfer cases)

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about motorcycle registration and licensing in Bangladesh

Q: Can I register a second-hand bike?

A: Yes, with ownership transfer form & original blue-book along with proper documentation from both parties.

Q: Can I apply for license online?

A: Yes, via BRTA portal. However, you'll still need to visit BRTA office for biometric verification and final submission.

Q: Is insurance mandatory for registration?

A: Yes, third-party insurance is required by law for all motor vehicles in Bangladesh before registration.

Q: What if I don't register my bike?

A: It's illegal. You may face fines up to ৳5,000, and your bike could be impounded by law enforcement.

Q: How long does the registration process take?

A: Usually 7–15 working days, depending on BRTA workload and completeness of your documents.
