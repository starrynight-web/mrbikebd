Yamaha Launches New FZ-X 2025 in Bangladesh

Published on June 17, 2025 · By Mr. Bike BD Team

The Yamaha FZ-X 2025 has officially launched in the Bangladeshi market, bringing with it a retro-inspired design blended with modern-day features.

The bike is powered by a 149cc air-cooled engine, producing 12.4 PS of power and 13.3 Nm of torque. It's mated to a 5-speed gearbox and comes with advanced features such as Bluetooth connectivity, LED headlamp, and a fully digital console.

Yamaha claims the FZ-X 2025 is designed for urban riders who seek both comfort and style. The seating posture is upright, and the suspension setup is tuned for city potholes and speed breakers.

In terms of pricing, the bike is expected to be priced around BDT 3,00,000. Bookings have already started at Yamaha showrooms across major cities like Dhaka, Chittagong, and Rajshahi.

Mr. Arefin, a Yamaha dealer from Dhaka, said: "This is a game-changer for those who want a mix of classic styling and modern tech. Youth will love it."
